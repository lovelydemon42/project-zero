About Us

Soul to Soul Interpreting, LLC was founded by Tabytha Garcia in December of 2019 in Palm Springs, California. Tabytha has a passion for communication and providing access to it. As of now she is the sole interpreter and CEO of Soul to Soul Interpreting, LLC. Her drive for herself and for her company is fueled only by her love for the languages she speaks, the community she serves, and the idea that at the end of the day she helped bridge a gap between two worlds that never needed to be seperated.
